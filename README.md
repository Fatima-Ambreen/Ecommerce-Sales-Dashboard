2. Short Description / Purpose

The Madhav E-Commerce Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report designed to provide insights into sales, profits, and product performance across cities and quarters. It helps users identify top customers, profitable categories, and trends in sales quantity and payment modes.

4. Data Source

The dashboard uses two main CSV files:

Order Table: Contains City, Customer Name, Order Date, Order ID, and State.

Details Table: Contains Sum of Amount, Category, Order ID, Payment Mode, Sum of Profit, Sum of Quantity, and Subcategory.

5. Features / Highlights

Business Problem:
Tracking sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends across multiple cities and quarters is difficult without an intuitive visual tool. Stakeholders need quick answers about top-performing customers, profitable categories, and payment trends.

Goal of the Dashboard:
To deliver an interactive Power BI dashboard that:

Highlights top customers, states, and product categories.

Visualizes profit, sales quantity, and payment mode trends.

Enables filtering by city and quarterly insights.

Walkthrough of Key Visuals:

Key KPIs (Cards): Quick insights into top metrics like total sales, profit, and quantities.

Bar Charts: Display the top 5 sub-categories by profit  and top 4 states by sales amount.

Column Charts: Show monthly profit trends and top 4 customers by sales amount .

Donut Charts: Highlight all categories by quantity and distribution of different payment modes by quantity.

Interactive Filters: Dropdown menus allow selection of cities, while tiles show sales for each quarter.

Business Impact & Insights:

Customer Analysis: Identify top 4 customers for focused marketing or loyalty programs.

Regional Performance: Understand top-performing states for expansion or targeted campaigns.

Category Insights: Discover high-profit product categories to optimize inventory and promotions.

Payment Trends: Analyze popular payment modes for operational improvements.

Quarterly Review: Track seasonal performance and make data-driven strategic decisions.
